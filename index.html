A "passion first" game group

Studio ECSP is an independent game developer group formed with a single goal: To promote artistic passion in this industry full of corporate slop, microtransactions and "profit first" mentality among game developers.
We won't lie: We also like profit. But we believe that our approach to getting profit should not disturb our creative output and integrity, we want our profits to be purely and exclusively because our customers love our arts. Videogames are art, not products.

OUR VALUES

ART OVER MONEY

We prioritize artistic integrity over economic benefit when it comes to creating our projects. We hate how a lot of industry giants have warped their franchises into lifeless products.

REJECT CORPO, RETURN TO HUMAN

Corporate and business etiquette restricts human expression. We act like a human business.

ARTISTS TOGETHER STRONG

We're very open to collaborate with other people or game developer teams that share our passion for videogames.
